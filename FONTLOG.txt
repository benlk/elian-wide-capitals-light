FONTLOG for Elian Wide Capitals Light EWCL_1

Basic Font Information

EWCL_1 is an ASCII typeface that supports languages using the Lating script, and could be expanded to support its variants for better UTF-8 support.

To contibute to this project, follow the instructions at https://github.com/benlk/elian-wide-capitals-light/blob/master/CONTRIBUTING.md
